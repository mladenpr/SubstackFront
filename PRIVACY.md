# Privacy Policy for Substack Front

**Last Updated:** January 16, 2026

## Overview

Substack Front is a browser extension that displays your Substack subscriptions in a magazine-style layout. This privacy policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it's stored, and your control over that data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

Substack Front extracts the following information from your Substack inbox page:

- Post titles and subtitles
- Publication names
- Cover image URLs
- Post URLs
- Publication dates
- Read/unread status (tracked locally)

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Your Substack account credentials
- Your email address or personal information
- Your browsing history outside of Substack
- Analytics or usage telemetry
- Any data for advertising purposes

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API. Your data:

- Never leaves your browser
- Is not transmitted to any external servers
- Is not shared with third parties
- Is not synced across devices

## Data Retention

Post data remains stored locally until you:

- Click the "Clear Posts" button in the extension
- Uninstall the extension
- Clear your browser's extension data

## Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Store posts and preferences locally |
| `tabs` | Open articles and refresh your feed |
| `host_permissions` (substack.com) | Read post data from your Substack inbox |

## Your Control

You have full control over your data:

- **View**: Open the new tab page or popup to see stored posts
- **Delete**: Click "Clear Posts" to remove all stored data
- **Disable**: Toggle "Popup Only Mode" to disable the new tab override

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services. All fonts are system fonts that are already installed on your device.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

Updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
